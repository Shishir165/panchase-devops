## What is Panchase?

Panchase is a biodiversity hotspot located in the foothills
of Pokhara, Nepal, spreading across 3 districts.

Home to rare flora and fauna, ancient forests, and traditional
farming communities, Panchase represents the heart of Nepal's
natural heritage.

## Our First Biodiversity Conference

We hosted our first biodiversity conference at the foothills
of Panchase, bringing together conservationists, farmers,
and tourism experts to shape the future of this unique ecosystem.

## Our Mission

To protect and promote the natural biodiversity of Panchase
while creating sustainable livelihoods through eco-friendly
tourism and organic farming.

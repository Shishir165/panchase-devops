## Organic Farm

Our organic farm at Panchase practices traditional Nepali
farming techniques combined with modern sustainable methods.
No chemicals, no pesticides — just pure organic produce
from the hills of Pokhara.

## Biodiversity Conservation

We document and protect the unique species found in the
Panchase region — from rare Himalayan birds to endemic
plant species found nowhere else on earth.

## Eco Friendly Tourism

Experience the real Nepal. Trek through ancient forests,
stay with local families, eat organic farm food, and
contribute directly to conservation efforts.
